Sponsor Meeting Information

Sponsor meetings are a vital part of many recovery programs. Creating a sponsor meeting will:

  • Schedule a Google Meet video call
  • Send an email invitation to your sponsor/sponsee
  • Create a calendar event with a reminder
  • Provide a private and secure meeting space
Sponsor meetings typically last 30-60 minutes and focus on specific recovery steps or challenges.
Meeting Preparation Tips
  • Prepare specific questions or topics
  • Review your step work progress
  • Note any challenges since last meeting
  • Consider writing down your discussion points
  • Find a private, quiet location for the call
