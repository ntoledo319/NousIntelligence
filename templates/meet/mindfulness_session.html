Mindfulness Session Information

Creating a mindfulness session will:

  • Schedule a Google Meet video call
  • Send email invitations to all participants

  • Create a calendar event for the session
Mindfulness sessions are typically 20-30 minutes in length for optimal focus and benefit.
Session Types
  • Guided Meditation: Structured meditation with verbal guidance
  • Breathing Exercises: Focused breathing techniques
  • Gentle Yoga: Mindful movement and stretching
  • Body Scanning: Progressive awareness of physical sensations
  • Stress Reduction: MBSR-based practices
  • Self-Compassion: Cultivating kindness toward self
  • Mindful Awareness: Open monitoring of experiences
