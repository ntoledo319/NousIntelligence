5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique

Use your senses to ground yourself in the present moment:

  • 5 See: Name 5 things you can see right now
  • 4 Touch: Notice 4 things you can feel/touch
  • 3 Hear: Identify 3 things you can hear
  • 2 Smell: Find 2 things you can smell
  • 1 Taste: Notice 1 thing you can taste

Take your time with each step. This helps bring your attention to the present moment.

De-escalation Techniques

Cold temperature can help reduce intense emotions:

  • Hold ice in your hands
  • Splash cold water on your face
  • Place a cold pack on your forehead
  • Take a cool shower

This activates the parasympathetic nervous system and helps calm your body's stress response.

Physical activity can help release tension:

  • Do jumping jacks for 1 minute
  • Run in place
  • Do wall push-ups
  • Squeeze a stress ball repeatedly

Exercise helps your body process stress hormones and can reduce emotional intensity.

Slow your breathing to activate your parasympathetic nervous system:

  1. Inhale slowly through your nose for 4 counts
  2. Hold your breath for 2 counts
  3. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 counts
  4. Repeat for at least 2 minutes

Making your exhale longer than your inhale helps activate your body's relaxation response.
