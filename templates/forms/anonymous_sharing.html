About Anonymous Sharing Forms

Anonymous sharing forms enable group participants to share thoughts, feelings, and experiences without revealing their identity.

Benefits
  • Increased psychological safety
  • More honest and open sharing
  • Reduces stigma and fear of judgment
  • Collects valuable group insights
  • Facilitates deeper therapeutic work
Features
  • No identifying information collected
  • Customized questions based on group type
  • Optional response sharing during sessions
  • Pre-meeting submission capability
  • Flexible sharing formats
