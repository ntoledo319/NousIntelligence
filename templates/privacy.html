Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

At NOUS, we believe your personal data should remain personal. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data We Collect

  • Account information (name, email) for authentication
  • Chat conversations to provide personalized assistance
  • Usage analytics to improve our services
  • Health and wellness data you choose to share

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized AI assistance
  • To improve our services and features
  • To ensure security and prevent misuse
  • To provide customer support when requested

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your information is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can also export your data or request account deletion through the settings panel.
